# Mobile-Sales-Dashboard

## This Project is based on the youtube video by Mr. Satish Dhawale: [Power BI Dashboard Tutorial for Beginners – Full Project with DAX | 2025 Practice Files](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WH937Gc_8eg) 
** Major Changes:
* Larger Data Volume
* Creation of Dimension & Fact tables using STAR schema
* Customer Age wise Segmentation
* Region clustering based on RegionCitiy mapping table
 
## Business Problem
Analyze mobile sales performance across brands, cities, customer segments, and payment methods to identify growth opportunities and customer purchasing patterns.

## Dataset
* 10,000 Transactions
* Multiple Mobile Brands
* Multiple Cities
* Customer Demographics
* Customer Ratings
* KPIs
* Total Revenue
* Units Sold
* Average Selling Price
* Average Customer Rating
* MTD Sales
* QTD Sales
* YTD Sales

## Visuals
* KPI Cards
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Brand Performance
* Model Performance
* City Performance
* Customer Age Analysis
* Payment Method Distribution
* Customer Ratings Analysis

## Key Insights
* Top Selling Brand
* Top Selling Mobile Model
* Highest Revenue City
* Most Preferred Payment Method
* Best Rated Mobile Brand
